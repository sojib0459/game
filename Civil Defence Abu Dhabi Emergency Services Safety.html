Historical Development of Safety Services

The roots of Abu Dhabi's emergency services go back to the early days. As the city grew, so did the need for strong safety measures. The Abu Dhabi Civil Defence Department was set up in 1976, starting a new era of safety.

Over the years, the services have kept up with Abu Dhabi's fast growth and new dangers. They've added new units, training, and equipment to stay ahead.
